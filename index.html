North and South

WED Game Jam Theme: Better Together

Welcome to the world of North and South!

This world is just like your own... Except! North and South are here!

Unfourtunately, they were seperated by an evil demon king, and were put at the opposite sides of the earth.

Now, to get back together again, they have to fight monsters and solve puzzles, and they must defeat the demonking!

About the Game

North and South is a 2D platformer game where you play as North and South, two characters who are trying to reunite at the equator.
